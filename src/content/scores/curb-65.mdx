## 🎯 Purpose

ประเมิน **severity ของ community-acquired pneumonia (CAP)** เพื่อตัดสินใจ **admit vs OPD**

**Range:** 0–5 points (1 point per criterion)

---

## 📊 Scoring (CURB-65 mnemonic)

| Letter | Criterion | Cutoff |
|---|---|---|
| **C** | **C**onfusion (new onset / AMS) | GCS &lt; 15 or disoriented |
| **U** | **U**rea (BUN) | &gt; 20 mg/dL (= 7 mmol/L) |
| **R** | **R**espiratory rate | ≥ 30 /min |
| **B** | **B**lood pressure | SBP &lt; 90 or DBP ≤ 60 |
| **65** | Age | ≥ 65 years |

**Each = 1 point, max 5**

**CRB-65** (without lab) = simpler version for community / prehospital

---

## 🚦 Interpretation — Disposition

| Score | 30-day Mortality | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| **0–1** | Low (~1.5%) | **OPD** / home treatment OK |
| **2** | Moderate (~9%) | **Admit** general ward |
| **3–5** | High (15–40%) | **Admit**, consider **ICU** (especially ≥ 4) |

### ICU indicators (regardless of CURB-65):
- Need mechanical ventilation
- Septic shock (vasopressor requirement)
- Plus 3+ of:
  - RR ≥ 30
  - PaO₂/FiO₂ &lt; 250
  - Multilobar infiltrates
  - Confusion/disorientation
  - BUN ≥ 20
  - WBC &lt; 4,000
  - Plt &lt; 100,000
  - Core temp &lt; 36°C
  - Hypotension needing fluid

---

## 💊 Empiric antibiotic (Thai community)

### OPD (CURB 0–1)
- **Amoxicillin 1 g PO TID × 5–7 d** (first-line)
- Alt: Doxycycline 100 mg BID
- If atypical suspected: + Azithromycin 500 mg day 1 then 250 mg × 4 d
- If PCN allergy: Levofloxacin 750 mg PO daily

### Admit ward (CURB 2)
- **Ceftriaxone 2 g IV q 24 hr + Azithromycin 500 mg IV daily**
- Alt: Levofloxacin 750 mg IV daily

### ICU (CURB ≥ 3 or severe)
- **Ceftriaxone 2 g IV q 24 hr + Azithromycin 500 mg IV daily**
- Add antipseudomonal if risk factors:
  - **Pip-Tazo 4.5 g IV q 8 hr** OR Cefepime 2 g IV q 8 hr
- Add Vancomycin if MRSA risk (healthcare exposure, severe)

### Adjust per local resistance + culture results

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

```
🚩 CURB-65 ≠ only criterion
   - Some low-score pts need admit (hypoxia, comorbidity)
   - Some high-score can be OPD (social, palliative)
   - Use clinical judgment

🚩 Elderly with pneumonia
   - Often atypical presentation (confusion, falls)
   - "Senior shift" — low threshold for admit

🚩 BUN cutoff 19 mg/dL is borderline
   - Round liberally to 20
   - Chronic CKD confounds

🚩 Don't delay antibiotics
   - First dose within 4 hr of admission (IDSA)
   - Within 1 hr if sepsis

🚩 Always check:
   - SpO₂ / ABG
   - Lactate (sepsis marker)
   - qSOFA (sepsis screen)
   - Blood cultures before abx
   - CXR / POCUS

🚩 HAP / VAP ≠ CAP
   - CURB-65 is for community
   - HAP: Pip-Tazo, Meropenem, add Vanco
   - Different pathogen spectrum

🚩 Influenza / COVID
   - Think viral CAP in season
   - Add antiviral per epidemiology
   - Different management
```
