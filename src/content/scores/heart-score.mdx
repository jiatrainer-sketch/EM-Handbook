## 🎯 Purpose

Risk stratify **undifferentiated chest pain** ที่ ED — ทำนาย **30-day MACE**
(Major Adverse Cardiac Event: MI, PCI, CABG, death)

แยก low-risk → OPD, intermediate → admit observation, high-risk → ACS workup

---

## 📊 Scoring (5 components, 0–2 each, total 0–10)

### H — History (clinical suspicion)

| Score | Finding |
|---|---|
| 0 | Slightly suspicious (atypical, non-anginal) |
| 1 | Moderately suspicious (mixed) |
| 2 | Highly suspicious (classic: substernal pressure, exertional, radiation, diaphoresis) |

### E — ECG

| Score | Finding |
|---|---|
| 0 | Normal |
| 1 | Non-specific repolarization (LBBB, LVH, early repolarization, pacemaker, non-specific ST/T changes) |
| 2 | Significant ST deviation (≥ 0.5 mm, new ischemic change) not from LBBB/LVH |

### A — Age

| Score | Age |
|---|---|
| 0 | &lt; 45 |
| 1 | 45–64 |
| 2 | ≥ 65 |

### R — Risk Factors

Count: HT, DM, current/past smoker, FHx early CAD, hyperlipidemia, obesity (BMI ≥ 30)

| Score | Finding |
|---|---|
| 0 | No known risk factor |
| 1 | 1–2 risk factors |
| 2 | ≥ 3 risk factors **or** known CAD (prior MI, PCI, CABG, stenosis ≥ 50%) |

### T — Troponin (initial, conventional assay)

| Score | Value |
|---|---|
| 0 | ≤ Normal limit |
| 1 | 1–3× upper normal |
| 2 | &gt; 3× upper normal |

---

## 🚦 Interpretation — 30-day MACE risk

| Score | Risk category | 30-day MACE | Disposition |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0–3** | Low | **~1.7%** | **Discharge** + OPD f/u ถ้า clinical fit |
| **4–6** | Moderate | **~16.6%** | **Admit observation** + serial troponin + stress test |
| **7–10** | High | **~50%** | **ACS workup**, cath lab referral |

### Additional safety checks before discharge (HEART 0–3)

- Serial troponin ถ้าอาการ &lt; 6 hr (HEART Pathway)
- Stable hemodynamics, no ongoing pain
- ECG stable
- ไม่มี red flag: syncope, heart failure, dynamic pain

---

## 💡 Clinical use

### HEART Pathway (modified)

- HEART 0–3 **+** troponin × 2 negative (0 + 3 hr) → discharge
- Saves ~40% of low-risk from unnecessary admit
- 30-day MACE 0.9–1.7% (acceptable miss rate per AHA)

### ≠ Substitute for clinical judgment

- Ongoing pain, unstable vitals → admit regardless
- Recent PCI / known CAD → be cautious
- Young patient + cocaine → workup anyway

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

```
🚩 History score ขึ้นกับ bias ของผู้ประเมิน
   - สอน intern: typical = substernal + exertional + radiation + diaphoresis
   - Atypical ใน elderly + DM + female
   - ถามซ้ำถ้าไม่ชัด

🚩 Troponin assay สำคัญ
   - Conventional troponin — spec นี้ใช้ cutoff 3×
   - High-sensitivity troponin ใช้ delta (change over time)
   - HEART score + hs-cTn = ปรับคำแนะนำ (see HEART Pathway)

🚩 ECG normal ≠ no ACS
   - 30% ของ ACS มี ECG ปกติ initially
   - Repeat ECG q 10–15 min ถ้า ongoing pain
   - Posterior leads ถ้าสงสัย

🚩 Score 4–6 = gray zone
   - ตัดสินใจจาก clinical + serial troponin
   - ไม่ automatic admit ทุกราย แต่อย่า discharge ไว

🚩 Known CAD = R score 2 อัตโนมัติ
   - อย่าลืม history stent, CABG, prior MI

🚩 Elderly atypical
   - Dyspnea, syncope, weakness = anginal equivalent
   - History score อาจต่ำ แต่ risk จริงสูง
   - ระวัง undertriage

🚩 อย่าใช้ HEART กับ STEMI
   - STEMI = cath lab ทันที ไม่ต้อง score
   - HEART สำหรับ undifferentiated / non-STEMI picture เท่านั้น
```
