## 🎯 Purpose

คัดกรอง **acute appendicitis** ในผู้ป่วย RLQ pain — ตัดสินใจ observe vs imaging vs surgical consult

**ใช้ใน:** ER triage ของ RLQ abdominal pain

---

## 📋 Criteria — **MANTRELS** (max 10)

| Letter | Criterion | Points |
|---|---|---|
| **M** | **M**igration of pain (umbilical → RLQ) | 1 |
| **A** | **A**norexia | 1 |
| **N** | **N**ausea / vomiting | 1 |
| **T** | **T**enderness in RLQ | **2** |
| **R** | **R**ebound tenderness | 1 |
| **E** | **E**levation of temperature (≥ 37.3°C) | 1 |
| **L** | **L**eukocytosis (WBC &gt; 10,000) | **2** |
| **S** | **S**hift to the left (neutrophil &gt; 75%) | 1 |

**Total: 0–10**

---

## 📊 Interpretation

| Score | Probability | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **1–4** | Low | Discharge with f/u; alternative dx |
| **5–6** | Intermediate | **Observation** or imaging (US/CT) |
| **7–8** | High | **Imaging** (US/CT) or surgical consult |
| **9–10** | Very high | **Surgical consult** (possibly straight to OR) |

### Modified Alvarado (MAS, score 0–9)
- Drop the "shift to the left" (often unavailable)
- Threshold for surgery: ≥ 7

---

## 🗺 Algorithm (pragmatic ED use)

```
RLQ pain → Alvarado

Score ≤ 4 → unlikely
  → Discharge with safety-net (return if worsen, 24 hr recheck)

Score 5–6 → equivocal
  → US (first-line — no radiation, cheap)
     ├── Positive → surgical consult
     ├── Negative but high suspicion → CT
     └── Indeterminate → observe or CT

Score 7–10 → likely
  → CT abdomen with contrast (adult)
     หรือ US (pediatric, pregnancy)
  → Surgical consult (often parallel)
```

---

## 👶 Population notes

### Children
- Alvarado + **PAS** (Pediatric Appendicitis Score) → better
- Low threshold for US (no radiation)
- Retrocecal appendix → may have minimal RLQ findings

### Female (reproductive age)
- **Broader differential**: ectopic, PID, ovarian torsion, ruptured cyst
- Need pelvic exam + β-hCG + pelvic US
- Alvarado lower sensitivity

### Elderly
- **Atypical** — less fever, less leukocytosis
- Higher perforation rate → low threshold for CT
- Mortality higher (delayed presentation)

### Pregnancy
- Pain may localize higher (appendix displaced upward)
- **MRI** preferred if US indeterminate (avoid CT radiation)

---

## 📝 Clinical Use — Example

**ผู้ป่วย:** ชาย 22 ปี, peri-umbilical pain → RLQ × 12 hr, N/V, anorexia, T 38.0, RLQ tenderness + rebound, WBC 14,000 neutro 82%

- M: 1, A: 1, N: 1, T: 2, R: 1, E: 1, L: 2, S: 1
- **Alvarado = 10 → very high → surgical consult + CT confirmation**

## ⚠️ ข้อควรระวัง (Critical Pitfalls)

<Callout type="danger" title="Alvarado Pitfalls">

**Low score = rule out** — ผิดใน subgroup  
→ Alvarado sensitivity ~88% — miss ~10% ที่ appendicitis จริง; ต้อง safety-net + recheck

**ใช้ Alvarado ในหญิง reproductive age เพียงอย่างเดียว** — lower specificity  
→ Always do pelvic exam + β-hCG + consider gyn causes

**ผู้สูงอายุใช้ score เดียวกัน** — atypical, less fever/WBC  
→ Low threshold for CT แม้ score กลาง

**Score สูง แต่รอ imaging** — อย่าให้ perforate  
→ Score 9–10 + classical presentation = surgery; imaging confirms ไม่ได้ delay surgery

**CT ไม่ได้ผล = no appendicitis** — check quality of study  
→ Poor contrast, collapsed appendix (can't see), retrocecal position → repeat or US หรือ surgical consult

**Alvarado ไม่ crystal ball** — score กลาง ยังต้อง imaging + serial exam  
→ ถ้าสงสัยแต่ score 5, observe 6 hr + repeat exam + labs มีประโยชน์กว่า discharge

</Callout>
