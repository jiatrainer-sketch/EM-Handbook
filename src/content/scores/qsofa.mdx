## 🎯 Purpose

**Bedside screening** สำหรับ **suspected infection** เพื่อระบุคนไข้เสี่ยงเสียชีวิตจาก sepsis — **ทำได้เร็วที่ ER / ward / pre-hospital**

ใช้แทน SIRS ใน sepsis-3 (2016). ไม่ใช่ diagnostic แต่เป็น **trigger for further assessment**.

---

## 📊 Scoring (1 point each)

| Criterion | Cutoff |
|---|---|
| **Respiratory rate** | **≥ 22 /min** |
| **Altered mental status** | GCS &lt; 15 (any change) |
| **Systolic BP** | **≤ 100 mmHg** |

**Range: 0–3 points**

---

## 🚦 Interpretation

| Score | Mortality risk | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0–1 | Low (&lt; 3%) | Continue evaluation per clinical judgment |
| **≥ 2** | **High (10–20%)** | **Suspect sepsis → start workup + treat** |

⚠️ **qSOFA ≥ 2 ≠ sepsis diagnosis** — triggers aggressive workup  
⚠️ **qSOFA 0 does NOT rule out sepsis** — sensitivity is moderate

---

## ⚡ ถ้า qSOFA ≥ 2 ทำอะไรต่อ

**Hour-1 bundle (Surviving Sepsis):**

1. **Measure lactate** (repeat if &gt; 2)
2. **Blood cultures × 2** before antibiotics
3. **Broad-spectrum antibiotics** within 1 hr
4. **Fluid** 30 mL/kg crystalloid (= 1,800 mL @ BW 60 kg) for hypotension or lactate ≥ 4
5. **Vasopressor** (norepinephrine) if MAP &lt; 65 after fluid

→ See **Septic Shock protocol** for full management

## 🔍 Compare qSOFA vs SIRS vs NEWS

| | qSOFA | SIRS | NEWS |
|---|---|---|---|
| Criteria count | 3 | 4 | 7 (weighted) |
| Use case | Predict mortality | Screen inflammation | General deterioration |
| Setting | Non-ICU | Any | Ward/ED |
| Sepsis-3 | Recommended | Replaced | Also useful |

---

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

```
🚩 qSOFA is SCREENING, not DIAGNOSIS
   - Positive = suspect, investigate
   - Negative = doesn't rule out
   - Always use clinical judgment

🚩 qSOFA sensitivity is moderate
   - Misses some septic patients
   - Use with clinical gestalt
   - Full SOFA if ICU

🚩 Altered mental = ANY change
   - Not just GCS < 15 strictly
   - Confusion, drowsiness, agitation counts
   - Baseline dementia: compare to baseline

🚩 Chronic conditions confound
   - Chronic low BP (Parkinson, elderly)
   - Chronic high RR (COPD)
   - Dementia (baseline altered)
   - Adjust threshold clinically

🚩 Don't delay treatment
   - qSOFA 2 + infection → act
   - Don't wait for blood culture result
   - Antibiotic within 1 hr
```
