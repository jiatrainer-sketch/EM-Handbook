## 🎯 Clinical Presentation

### Pulmonary TB
- **Classic**: chronic cough ≥ 2 wk, hemoptysis, night sweats, weight loss, fever
- **Atypical (esp. HIV)**: normal CXR possible; disseminated; extrapulmonary

### CXR findings
- Upper lobe cavitation / consolidation (reactivation)
- Miliary pattern (disseminated TB)
- Lower lobe / middle lobe (HIV, elderly — primary pattern)
- Hilar lymphadenopathy
- Pleural effusion

### Extrapulmonary sites
| Site | Presentation |
|---|---|
| **Lymph node** (scrofula) | Cervical LN enlargement, painless, matted |
| **Pleural** | Exudative effusion, ADA &gt; 40, lymphocytic |
| **Meningitis** | Subacute headache, CN palsy, basilar enhancement |
| **Spine** (Pott) | Back pain, gibbus, cold abscess |
| **GU** | Sterile pyuria, renal scarring |
| **GI** | Ileocecal mass, ascites (TB peritonitis) |
| **Pericardial** | Constrictive pericarditis |

## 🧪 Diagnosis

### Sputum-based
- **AFB smear × 3** (early morning, 8-hr apart — but 2 specimens now acceptable)
- **Gene Xpert MTB/RIF** — best initial test (1–2 hr, detects TB + rifampicin resistance)
- **Mycobacterial culture** — gold standard (6–8 weeks; detects drug resistance)

### Imaging
- **CXR** — baseline for all
- **CT chest** — if CXR equivocal; assess cavitary/miliary
- **MRI brain/spine** — TB meningitis, Pott's disease

### Blood tests
- **IGRA** (QuantiFERON, T-SPOT.TB) — LTBI vs active; does NOT differentiate
- **TST (tuberculin skin test)** — similar to IGRA; less specific (BCG affects)

### Extrapulmonary
- **Pleural fluid**: ADA &gt; 40 U/L, lymphocytic exudate; biopsy highest yield
- **CSF**: ADA, PCR, culture; MRI enhancement
- **Lymph node biopsy**: FNA or excisional; AFB + PCR + histology (caseating granuloma)

<Callout type="warning" title="Gene Xpert = game changer in Thai ED">

- ใช้ใน community hospital ได้
- Detects TB + rifampicin resistance ใน 2 hr
- Sensitivity ~85% (smear +) / ~65% (smear − culture +)
- **ถ้าสงสัย TB → ส่ง Xpert + sputum C/S ทุกราย**

</Callout>

## 💊 Treatment — First-line (DS-TB)

### Standard regimen (WHO/Thai MOPH)

```
Intensive phase (2 เดือน): HRZE
  H — Isoniazid
  R — Rifampicin
  Z — Pyrazinamide
  E — Ethambutol

Continuation phase (4 เดือน): HR
```

**Total duration: 6 months** สำหรับ drug-sensitive pulmonary TB

### Drug doses (daily, adult)

| ยา | Dose | Max |
|---|---|---|
| **Isoniazid (H)** | 5 mg/kg | 300 mg |
| **Rifampicin (R)** | 10 mg/kg | 600 mg |
| **Pyrazinamide (Z)** | 25 mg/kg | 2,000 mg |
| **Ethambutol (E)** | 15 mg/kg | 1,200 mg |

**Fixed-dose combinations** (Thailand uses FDC): 4-drug or 2-drug tablets ตาม BW

### Extended regimens
| Site | Total Duration |
|---|---|
| Pulmonary (most) | 6 months (HRZE 2 + HR 4) |
| TB meningitis | 9–12 months (HRZE 2 + HR 7–10) + steroids |
| Bone/joint | 6–9 months |
| Disseminated / miliary | 6–9 months |
| TB + HIV | 6 months; start ART within 2 wk (8 wk if TBM) |

### Corticosteroids — adjunctive
<Callout type="info" title="Steroids ที่มี evidence">

- **TB meningitis**: Dexamethasone 0.4 mg/kg/day → taper 8 weeks (reduces mortality)
- **TB pericarditis**: Prednisolone 60 mg/day → taper (controversial in HIV+)
- ไม่ routine ใน pulmonary TB

</Callout>

## 💊 Side Effects & Monitoring

| Drug | Side effect | Monitor |
|---|---|---|
| **Isoniazid** | Hepatotoxicity, peripheral neuropathy | LFT, **add Pyridoxine 25–50 mg/day** |
| **Rifampicin** | Hepatotoxicity, orange urine, drug interactions | LFT; **decreases OCP, warfarin, HIV drugs** |
| **Pyrazinamide** | Hepatotoxicity, hyperuricemia, arthralgia | LFT, uric acid |
| **Ethambutol** | Optic neuritis (dose-dependent) | **Visual acuity + color vision monthly** |

### Baseline labs
- LFT (AST/ALT/ALP/bilirubin)
- CBC, Cr, uric acid
- HIV test (CRUCIAL — co-infection common)
- HBsAg, HCV Ab
- Pregnancy test (women)
- Visual acuity + color vision (before ethambutol)

### Monitor during Rx
- **Clinical**: cough, weight, constitutional symptoms monthly
- **Sputum**: month 2 (if smear+); month 5 (failure screen)
- **LFT**: baseline, 2 wk, 4 wk, then monthly if normal; more often if symptoms
- **If ALT &gt; 3× ULN + symptomatic** or **&gt; 5× ULN asymptomatic** → hold all TB drugs, investigate

## 🦠 MDR-TB / RR-TB

### Definitions
- **RR-TB** (Rifampicin-Resistant): Xpert detects RIF resistance
- **MDR-TB**: resistant to ≥ Isoniazid + Rifampicin
- **Pre-XDR**: MDR + resistance to FQ or second-line injectable
- **XDR**: MDR + resistance to FQ + any second-line injectable

### Thailand MDR-TB regimen (WHO shorter-course, 9–11 months)
- Only if no previous exposure to second-line, no XDR
- **Bedaquiline + Linezolid + Levofloxacin/Moxifloxacin + Clofazimine + Cycloserine + Pyrazinamide + Ethambutol**

**Refer all MDR-TB cases to TB referral center (e.g., Bangrak Hospital, regional TB center)**

## 👥 TB + HIV

<Callout type="danger" title="TB + HIV = co-manage urgently">

- **HIV test ทุก TB patient**
- CD4 guides ART timing:
  - CD4 &lt; 50 → start ART within **2 weeks** of TB Rx
  - CD4 ≥ 50 → start ART within 8 weeks
  - TB meningitis → delay ART 4–8 wk (IRIS risk ↓↓)
- **Drug interactions**: Rifampicin ↓ many ART drugs → switch to Rifabutin OR adjust ART (efavirenz preferred)
- Cotrimoxazole prophylaxis if CD4 &lt; 200

</Callout>

## 🏥 Infection Control

- **Airborne isolation** (negative pressure room + N95) until:
  - On effective Rx ≥ 2 weeks AND
  - Clinical improvement AND
  - 3 consecutive negative AFB smears
- **Contact tracing** — household members + close contacts
- **Latent TB treatment** for contacts (INH 9 months OR 3HP regimen)

---

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

<Callout type="danger" title="TB Pitfalls">

**"Smear negative = no TB"** — sensitivity ~50%  
→ ถ้า clinical + CXR สงสัย → Xpert + culture + empirical Rx

**ไม่เริ่ม Pyridoxine กับ INH** — peripheral neuropathy preventable  
→ Pyridoxine 25–50 mg/day คู่ INH ทุกราย

**Drug interactions ของ Rifampicin** — induces CYP3A4 มาก  
→ Warfarin, OCP, HIV drugs, DOACs, corticosteroid — ต้อง review

**LFT ขึ้นเล็กน้อย หยุดยาทันที** — asymptomatic ALT &lt; 5× ULN → continue, monitor  
→ Hold only ถ้า symptomatic OR ALT &gt; 5× ULN

**TB + HIV: delay ART** — ทำให้ mortality สูงขึ้น  
→ Start ART within 2 wk if CD4 &lt; 50

**TB meningitis: ไม่ใช้ steroid** — evidence ชัดว่า dexamethasone ลด mortality  
→ Dexa 0.4 mg/kg/day → 8-wk taper เสมอ

</Callout>
