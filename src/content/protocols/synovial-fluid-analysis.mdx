## 🎯 Indications

<Callout type="danger" title="Acute monoarthritis = arthrocentesis ทุกราย">

Septic arthritis = **emergency** → joint destruction within 24–48 hr ถ้าไม่ได้ drainage + antibiotic

</Callout>

### Must-do paracentesis
- Acute monoarthritis (&lt; 2 สัปดาห์) — R/O septic
- Suspected septic arthritis (fever + joint pain + swelling)
- Diagnostic question: crystal vs infection
- Therapeutic drainage (hemarthrosis, large effusion)

### Contraindications (relative)
- Overlying skin infection at needle site (risk introduction)
- Bacteremia (cần เปลี่ยน site, clean skin thoroughly)
- Severe coagulopathy (correct if possible)

### Common approached joints
- **Knee**: lateral midpatellar, suprapatellar, or infrapatellar
- **Shoulder**: posterior approach (posterior to humeral head)
- **Wrist**: dorsal
- **Ankle**: anterolateral
- Ultrasound guidance ช่วยมากใน small joints / obese patients

---

## 🧪 Tests ที่ส่ง

### Routine (ทุกราย)
- **Cell count + differential** (WBC + % neutrophil — key!)
- **Gram stain + Culture** (bacterial)
- **Crystals** (polarized light microscopy)

### Optional
- **Glucose, LDH, protein** (มักไม่ช่วยแยก)
- **AFB + TB culture** (suspected TB arthritis)
- **Fungal culture** (chronic monoarthritis)
- **PCR for Neisseria gonorrhoeae** (young sexually active)
- **Lyme serology** (exposure history)

## 📊 WBC Interpretation

| WBC/mm³ | PMN % | Likely Dx |
|---|---|---|
| **&lt; 200** | &lt; 25% | Normal |
| **200–2,000** | &lt; 25% | Non-inflammatory (OA, trauma) |
| **2,000–50,000** | ≥ 50% | Inflammatory (gout, RA, pseudogout, reactive) |
| **&gt; 50,000** | &gt; 75% | **Septic arthritis** (assume until proven otherwise) |
| **&gt; 100,000** | &gt; 90% | Septic arthritis (high sensitivity) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Septic arthritis cutoffs ไม่ absolute">

- WBC 30,000–50,000 + PMN &gt; 75% + clinical suspicion = treat as septic  
- Crystal arthritis สามารถมี WBC &gt; 50,000 ได้ (pseudogout particularly)  
- ถ้าสงสัย septic แม้ WBC ต่ำ → **ให้ antibiotic + consult ortho**  
- Prosthetic joint: WBC &gt; 1,700 หรือ PMN &gt; 65% = septic (threshold ต่ำกว่า)

</Callout>

## 🦠 Septic Arthritis

### Common organisms
- **Staphylococcus aureus** (most common in all ages, incl. MRSA)
- **Streptococci** (groups A, B, pneumoniae)
- **N. gonorrhoeae** (young sexually active — disseminated gonococcal infection)
- **Gram-negative** (elderly, IV drug users, immunocompromised)
- **Salmonella** (sickle cell disease)
- **Kingella kingae** (children &lt; 4 yr)

### Empirical antibiotics
| Scenario | Regimen |
|---|---|
| **Adults, unknown** | Ceftriaxone 2 g IV q24h + Vancomycin 25 mg/kg IV (MRSA cover) |
| **Young, sexually active** | Ceftriaxone 1 g IV q24h (cover GC) |
| **IV drug user** | Vancomycin + Cefepime or Pip-tazo (GN + Pseudomonas) |
| **Prosthetic joint** | Vancomycin + Ceftriaxone/Pip-tazo; **consult orthopedic** |
| **Kids** | Ceftriaxone + Vancomycin; add Kingella coverage if &lt; 4 yr |

**Duration**: 2–4 weeks IV (transition to PO after clinical improvement, CRP trending down)

### Drainage — **essential!**
- **Needle aspiration** repeated daily (knees, accessible joints)
- **Arthroscopic washout** or **open surgical drainage** (hip, shoulder, failure of needle drainage, infected prosthesis)
- Daily aspiration + synovial fluid trend (WBC ลด = response)

## 💎 Crystal Arthritis

### Gout (Monosodium Urate — MSU)
- **Polarized light**: needle-shaped, **strongly negative birefringent** (yellow parallel to slow ray)
- Acute Rx: NSAID (naproxen 500 mg BID) + colchicine 1.2 → 0.6 mg 1 hr later + prednisolone 30–40 mg × 5 days (choose 1)
- Long-term: Allopurinol 100 mg start → titrate (target serum urate &lt; 6 mg/dL)

### Pseudogout (CPPD)
- **Polarized light**: rhomboid, **weakly positive birefringent**
- Acute Rx: NSAID, colchicine, intra-articular steroid
- ไม่มี urate-lowering therapy

<Callout type="info" title="Crystal + septic — ไม่แยกกันเลย!">

~5% ของ septic arthritis มี crystals ด้วย  
→ ถ้าไข้ + crystal + high WBC → **treat empirically as septic** until culture negative

</Callout>

## 🫁 TB Arthritis

- Chronic monoarthritis + constitutional symptoms
- Hip, knee most common; spine (Pott's disease) = separate
- Fluid: **WBC 10,000–20,000, lymphocytic, low glucose**
- AFB smear sensitivity low; **culture / PCR / synovial biopsy** needed
- Rx: HRZE × 2 months → HR × 7–10 months (longer than pulmonary)

---

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

<Callout type="danger" title="Arthrocentesis Pitfalls">

**Delay arthrocentesis เพราะรอ MRI** — ไม่จำเป็น  
→ Aspirate ก่อน, antibiotic หลัง sending culture

**Empirical antibiotic ก่อน aspirate** — อาจ sterilize culture  
→ Aspirate ก่อนเสมอ (ถ้ารอได้); ถ้าชะลอไม่ได้ → ให้ยาก่อน แต่ส่ง culture ทันที

**Crystals + high WBC = not septic** — ผิด! crystal arthritis + septic co-exist ได้ 5%  
→ Rx empirical abx + await culture

**ไม่คิดถึง GC ใน young patient** — disseminated gonococcal arthritis common  
→ Ceftriaxone + PCR for GC/Chlamydia

**Prosthetic joint — threshold ต่ำกว่า** — WBC &gt; 1,700 = septic  
→ Consult ortho STAT; do not inject into prosthetic joint empirically

</Callout>
