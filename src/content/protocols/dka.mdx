## 🎯 Criteria / Diagnosis

### DKA diagnostic triad

1. **Hyperglycemia**: BS &gt; 250 mg/dL (euglycemic DKA ถ้า SGLT2i, pregnancy, starvation — BS อาจปกติ)
2. **Ketosis**: serum / urine ketone positive (β-hydroxybutyrate &gt; 3 mmol/L)
3. **Metabolic acidosis**: pH &lt; 7.3 **หรือ** HCO₃ &lt; 18 **+** anion gap &gt; 12

### Severity (ADA)

| | Mild | Moderate | Severe |
|---|---|---|---|
| pH | 7.25–7.30 | 7.00–7.24 | **&lt; 7.00** |
| HCO₃ | 15–18 | 10–14 | &lt; 10 |
| Mental | Alert | Drowsy | **Stupor / coma** |
| AG | &gt; 10 | &gt; 12 | &gt; 12 |

### DKA vs HHS (Hyperosmolar Hyperglycemic State)

| | **DKA** | **HHS** |
|---|---|---|
| BS | &gt; 250 | **&gt; 600** |
| pH | &lt; 7.3 | &gt; 7.3 |
| HCO₃ | &lt; 18 | &gt; 18 |
| Ketone | Large | Small / none |
| Osm | &lt; 320 | **&gt; 320** |
| Onset | Hours–days | Days–weeks |
| Type | T1DM (mostly) | T2DM elderly |
| Mortality | ~1–5% | **~10–20%** |

⚠️ **Mixed DKA-HHS** พบบ่อยใน T2DM — treat as DKA

### Common precipitants (5 I's)

- **Infection** (40–50%) — UTI, pneumonia, cellulitis
- **Insulin** miss / pump failure / non-adherence
- **Ischemia / Infarction** — AMI, stroke, mesenteric
- **Intoxication** — alcohol, cocaine, glucocorticoid
- **Initial** presentation of DM (T1DM)

## ⚡ การรักษาทันที (Immediate Actions)

### Priority 1: ABC + initial assessment

- Airway, O₂, IV × 2
- BS, ABG/VBG, electrolyte (esp **K**), ketone, anion gap
- CBC, BUN/Cr, lactate, urinalysis
- 12-lead ECG (r/o MI + K-related changes)
- Search cause (CXR, blood culture, UA, lipase, etc.)

### Priority 2: **Fluid resuscitation (first)**

Fluid ก่อน insulin — insulin on top of hypovolemia = shock

**Hour 1–2: NSS 1–1.5 L IV rapid**
- Start 15–20 mL/kg (= 1 L @ 60 kg) over first hour
- Then 250–500 mL/hr × 1–2 hr more

**Hour 2+: adjust per corrected Na**
- Corrected Na = measured Na + 1.6 × [(BS − 100) / 100]
- **Corrected Na normal/high**: switch to **½ NSS** @ 250–500 mL/hr
- **Corrected Na low**: continue **NSS** @ 250–500 mL/hr

**When BS &lt; 200**: switch to **D5 ½ NSS** or add D5 to current fluid → keep BS 150–200

Total deficit ~100 mL/kg (~6 L @ 60 kg) — replace over 24–48 hr

⚠️ **Elderly / CHF / CKD**: smaller boluses, monitor for overload, use POCUS IVC

### Priority 3: **Check K before insulin!**

```
K < 3.3  →  HOLD insulin, give K 20–30 mEq/hr IV first, recheck
K 3.3–5.3 →  Start insulin + add K 20–40 mEq to each liter of fluid
K > 5.3  →  Start insulin, no K yet, recheck q 2 hr
```

### Priority 4: Insulin IV infusion

**Regular insulin drip:**
- **0.1 U/kg/hr IV infusion = 6 U/hr @ BW 60 kg**
- Alternative: **0.14 U/kg/hr no bolus** = 8 U/hr (newer ADA recommendation — avoids hypoK)
- ⚠️ IV bolus 0.1 U/kg (6 U) เป็น optional — **ไม่ควรให้ถ้า K &lt; 3.3**

**Target BS drop: 50–75 mg/dL/hr**
- ถ้าตกเร็วเกิน → risk cerebral edema (pediatric)
- ถ้าไม่ตก ใน 1 hr → double rate

**When BS &lt; 200**: **reduce insulin to 0.05 U/kg/hr** (= 3 U/hr @ 60 kg) + start **D5 fluid**  
→ keep BS 150–200 until AG closed

### Priority 5: Bicarbonate — **only if pH &lt; 6.9**

⚠️ Routine bicarb ไม่แนะนำ (ADA)

**ถ้า pH &lt; 6.9:**
- NaHCO₃ 100 mmol ใน 400 mL sterile water + KCl 20 mEq
- Infuse over 2 hr
- Recheck pH, repeat q 2 hr จน pH &gt; 7.0

**Risks of bicarb:**
- Paradoxical CNS acidosis
- Hypokalemia (K shifts back into cells)
- Cerebral edema (peds)

### Priority 6: Transition to SC insulin

**Criteria to transition (ALL):**
- **Anion gap closed** (&lt; 12)
- **HCO₃ &gt; 15**
- **pH &gt; 7.3**
- **Able to eat PO**

**Transition protocol:**
1. Calculate total daily SC insulin (TDD):
   - New DM: 0.5–0.8 U/kg/day (= 30–48 U @ 60 kg)
   - Known DM: use home regimen or TDD from last 24 hr
2. Split: 50% basal (glargine/NPH) + 50% prandial (regular/rapid)
3. **Overlap IV drip × 1–2 hr after first SC** — critical to prevent rebound DKA!
4. Give first SC with meal
5. Stop IV drip only AFTER SC active

## 💊 การเตรียมยา (Drug Preparation, BW 60 kg)

### Insulin IV infusion

```
Prep:     Regular insulin 50 U + NSS 50 mL
          = 1 U/mL (clear labeling — high-alert drug)
          
Rate:     0.1 U/kg/hr = 6 U/hr @ BW 60 kg
          → 6 mL/hr (at 1 U/mL)

Bolus:    0.1 U/kg = 6 U IV push (optional)
          Skip bolus if K < 3.3 OR using 0.14 U/kg/hr approach

When BS < 200:
          Reduce to 0.05 U/kg/hr = 3 U/hr
          (= 3 mL/hr)
          + Start D5 containing fluid
```

⚠️ **High-alert drug** — double-check dose, prime tubing (insulin binds to plastic → first 20 mL to prime)

---

### Fluid resuscitation

```
Hour 1:   NSS 1 L IV (15–20 mL/kg)
Hour 2:   NSS 500 mL–1 L
          Then 250–500 mL/hr based on:
          - Corrected Na
          - Hemodynamics
          - Urine output

Fluid choice after hour 2:
  Corrected Na high/normal  →  ½ NSS 250–500 mL/hr
  Corrected Na low          →  NSS 250–500 mL/hr
  
When BS < 200  →  Add D5 OR switch to D5 ½ NSS
```

Total deficit ~6 L @ 60 kg, replace over 24–48 hr

---

### Potassium replacement

```
K < 3.3    →  HOLD insulin
              KCl 20–30 mEq/hr IV (central preferred)
              Recheck q 1 hr
              
K 3.3–5.3  →  Add KCl 20–40 mEq per L of maintenance fluid
              (target K 4–5)
              Recheck q 2–4 hr
              
K > 5.3    →  No K in fluid
              Recheck q 2 hr
```

⚠️ Max peripheral rate: KCl 10 mEq/hr (phlebitis); central up to 20–40 mEq/hr

---

### Bicarbonate (pH &lt; 6.9 only)

```
NaHCO₃ 100 mmol + sterile water 400 mL + KCl 20 mEq
Infuse over 2 hr
Recheck pH q 2 hr, repeat until pH > 7.0
```

⚠️ Not routine — only severe acidosis

## ⚠️ ข้อควรระวัง (Critical Pitfalls)

<Callout type="danger" title="DKA Pitfalls ที่ฆ่าคนไข้ได้">

**Fluid ก่อน, insulin ทีหลัง** — insulin ขณะ hypovolemia = shock

**ตรวจ K ก่อนให้ insulin เสมอ** — K &lt; 3.3 → หยุด insulin + แก้ K ก่อน  
→ Insulin + hypoK = fatal arrhythmia

**อย่าหยุด insulin เมื่อ BS ปกติ** — ปิด anion gap ก่อน (AG &lt; 12)  
→ เปลี่ยนเป็น rate ต่ำ + เพิ่ม D5W แทน

**หยุด IV insulin ผิดวิธี = rebound DKA** — t½ IV insulin = 5 นาที  
→ ให้ SC ก่อน 1–2 hr แล้วค่อยหยุด IV

**Euglycemic DKA** (SGLT2i, ตั้งครรภ์) — BS อาจปกติ &lt; 250  
→ ตรวจ AG + ketone ทุกคนที่ใช้ SGLT2i + มี N/V

**หาสาเหตุกระตุ้น** — UTI, ปอดอักเสบ, MI, pancreatitis  
→ UA + culture, CXR, ECG, troponin, lipase ทุกราย

</Callout>

## 📝 คำสั่งพร้อมใช้ (Copy-Ready Order)

```
1. ABC, O₂ prn, IV × 2 (large bore)
2. NPO, NG tube if vomiting / altered mental
3. Continuous ECG monitor
4. Foley catheter — strict I/O hourly
5. Labs STAT + q 2–4 hr:
   - BS (bedside q 1 hr)
   - VBG or ABG q 2–4 hr
   - BMP q 2–4 hr (K, Na, HCO₃, Cr, AG)
   - CBC, LFT, lipase, lactate, phosphate
   - Urine/serum ketone, β-hydroxybutyrate
6. Precipitant workup:
   - CXR
   - Urinalysis + culture
   - Blood culture × 2 (if febrile)
   - 12-lead ECG + troponin
   - Lipase / amylase
   - Pregnancy test (if applicable)
7. FLUID:
   - Hour 1: NSS 1 L IV (15–20 mL/kg)
   - Hour 2+: NSS or ½ NSS 250–500 mL/hr based on
     corrected Na + hemodynamics
   - Switch to D5-containing when BS < 200
8. POTASSIUM (check K before insulin):
   - K < 3.3: HOLD insulin, KCl 20–30 mEq/hr IV
   - K 3.3–5.3: KCl 20–40 mEq per L fluid
   - K > 5.3: no K, recheck q 2 hr
9. INSULIN drip:
   - Regular insulin 50 U + NSS 50 mL (= 1 U/mL)
   - Start 0.1 U/kg/hr = 6 U/hr (6 mL/hr @ BW 60 kg)
   - When BS < 200: reduce to 0.05 U/kg/hr
   - Target BS drop 50–75 mg/dL/hr
10. BICARB: only if pH < 6.9
    - NaHCO₃ 100 mmol in 400 mL SW + KCl 20 mEq over 2 hr
11. Transition to SC insulin when:
    - AG < 12, HCO₃ > 15, pH > 7.3, eating
    - TDD 0.5–0.8 U/kg (basal 50% + prandial 50%)
    - OVERLAP IV drip × 1–2 hr after first SC
12. Admit ICU if:
    - pH < 7.0, altered mental, shock, comorbidity
    - Otherwise: step-down/ward w/ close monitor
13. Treat precipitant (antibiotic, PCI, etc.)
14. Diabetic educator before D/C
```
