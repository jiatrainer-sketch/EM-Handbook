## 🎯 Definition

**Life-threatening adrenal insufficiency** — acute cortisol (± aldosterone) deficiency → circulatory collapse

**Rule:** ถ้าสงสัย → **ให้ Hydrocortisone ก่อน** รอ lab ไม่ได้

---

## 🔴 Clinical Presentation

### Classic triad
- **Hypotension** (refractory to fluid/pressors)
- **Abdominal pain** (นึกว่า surgical abdomen)
- **Altered mental status**

### Lab findings (supportive, ไม่ต้องรอ)
- **Hyponatremia** (↓ aldo + SIADH)
- **Hyperkalemia** (↓ aldo)
- **Hypoglycemia** (↓ cortisol)
- **Hypercalcemia** (mild)
- Metabolic acidosis
- Eosinophilia, lymphocytosis
- ↑ BUN (prerenal)

### Other symptoms
- Fever, chills (adrenal infection or sepsis)
- Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea
- Generalized weakness, fatigue
- Hyperpigmentation (chronic primary AI — Addison's)
- Postural hypotension

---

## 🔴 Precipitants

### 1. Known AI + stress (not enough stress dose)

- **Infection** (UTI, pneumonia, GI, sepsis) — most common
- **Surgery, trauma, burn**
- **Missed oral dose** (vomiting, NPO)
- **Dehydration, heat stroke**
- **MI, PE, stroke**
- **Pregnancy, labor**

### 2. New/undiagnosed AI

- **Autoimmune** (Addison's — most common primary)
- **Bilateral adrenal hemorrhage** (Waterhouse-Friderichsen: sepsis, esp. meningococcal, anticoag)
- **Infection:** TB, CMV (HIV), fungal
- **Chronic steroid use → abrupt withdrawal** (iatrogenic secondary AI)
- **Pituitary/hypothalamic disease** (Sheehan, apoplexy)
- **Drugs:** ketoconazole, etomidate, mitotane, opioids (chronic)

## 🔴 Immediate Actions (first 15 min — DON'T DELAY STEROID)

### Step 1 — GIVE STEROID IMMEDIATELY

```
Hydrocortisone 100 mg IV bolus — STAT
```

⚠️ **ไม่ต้องรอ** cortisol/ACTH level ถ้าจะช่วยชีวิต

If hydrocortisone not available:
- **Methylprednisolone 40 mg IV** (weaker MC effect)
- **Dexamethasone 4 mg IV** (ไม่ขัด cortisol assay — ถ้าจำเป็นต้อง confirm dx ภายหลัง)

### Step 2 — Fluid resuscitation

```
NSS 1 L IV rapid (15-30 min)
Then 2-3 L over next 12-24 hr
Add D5W if hypoglycemia
```

⚠️ ระวัง pulmonary edema ใน elderly / HF — guide by clinical + IVC US

### Step 3 — Treat hypoglycemia

```
D50W 50 mL IV push ถ้า glucose < 70
Then D5NSS หรือ D5½NSS maintain
```

### Step 4 — Treat hyperkalemia

- **ถ้า K > 6.5 หรือ ECG changes:** Ca gluconate 10 mL 10% IV + insulin-dextrose
- ปกติ K ลดเองหลัง hydrocortisone + fluid
- ดู hyperkalemia protocol

### Step 5 — Treat precipitant

- Blood culture × 2, urine culture, CXR
- **Empiric antibiotic** ถ้าสงสัย sepsis (broad spectrum)
- CT abdomen ถ้าสงสัย adrenal hemorrhage

### Step 6 — Labs (during/after steroid)

- **Cortisol, ACTH** (baseline — for later confirmation)
- CBC, electrolytes, glucose, BUN/Cr, Ca
- Blood culture × 2
- UA + culture
- ECG (hyperK changes)
- CXR
- Consider: TSH, thyroid (polyglandular syndrome)
- Consider: cortisol assay dexamethasone ก่อน

## 💊 การเตรียมยา (Drug Preparation, BW 60 kg)

### Hydrocortisone (first-line)

```
Amp: Hydrocortisone 100 mg/vial (reconstitute)

STAT loading: 100 mg IV bolus
Then 24-hr maintenance (2 options):
  (a) 50 mg IV q 6 hr (total 200 mg/day), OR
  (b) Continuous drip: 200 mg + NSS 50 mL → 8.3 mg/hr
      = 2 mL/hr via infusion pump

Day 2-3: taper to 100 mg/day (if clinically improving)
Day 4+: transition to PO maintenance 15-25 mg/day
       (e.g., 10 mg AM + 5 mg PM)
```

### Dexamethasone (alternative — doesn't interfere cortisol assay)

```
Dexamethasone 4 mg IV q 12 hr (equivalent ~ 100 mg hydrocortisone)
⚠️ No mineralocorticoid effect — must add fludrocortisone if primary AI
```

### Fludrocortisone (after stable, oral mineralocorticoid)

```
Fludrocortisone 0.05-0.2 mg PO daily
(Usually 0.1 mg daily, adjust per BP, K, renin)
⚠️ Not needed acutely ถ้า hydrocortisone ≥ 50 mg/day 
   (hydrocortisone has enough MC activity at high dose)
```

### Stress dose rules (for education/discharge)

```
Minor illness (fever, mild): 2-3× home dose x 2-3 days
Moderate (vomiting, unable PO): IM hydrocortisone 100 mg
  → ER ทันที
Major surgery/trauma/ICU: 100 mg q 8 hr IV
Emergency injection kit: give IM 100 mg + call for help
```

### ⚠️ Renal / Elderly

- ไม่ต้อง adjust dose ใน CKD
- Elderly: เริ่มที่ 50 mg q 6 hr, titrate ตาม response
- Watch for fluid overload, hyperglycemia

## ⚠️ ข้อควรระวัง (Critical Pitfalls)

```
🚩 ถ้าสงสัย → ให้ steroid ทันที
   - อย่ารอ cortisol level
   - รอ = คนไข้เสียชีวิต
   - Dexamethasone = option ถ้ายังต้อง confirm dx
     (ไม่รบกวน cortisol assay)

🚩 Hydrocortisone preferred > methylpred, dex
   - มี mineralocorticoid activity (glucose, Na, K)
   - ใน primary AI ต้องการทั้ง GC + MC

🚩 ระวัง fluid overload
   - 2-3 L ใน 24 hr แรก
   - Elderly / HF → guide by IVC, UO, pulmonary exam
   - อย่า over-correct hyponatremia > 8-10 mEq/day 
     (central pontine myelinolysis risk)

🚩 Hypoglycemia common
   - Check glucose ทันที + q 1-2 hr
   - D50 push ถ้า < 70
   - D5NSS maintenance

🚩 Hyperkalemia มักหายเอง
   - Hydrocortisone + fluid = treats
   - ถ้า K > 6.5 หรือ ECG → Ca + insulin-dex
   - ไม่ใช่ indication HD ส่วนใหญ่

🚩 Fever ≠ adrenal function
   - Fever ใน adrenal crisis = precipitant (infection)
   - Workup + empiric abx
   - Blood cx × 2, UA, CXR

🚩 Steroid "withdrawal crisis" = common
   - ผู้ป่วย chronic steroid (> 3 wk at prednisolone ≥ 5 mg/day)
   - Can develop secondary AI
   - Don't stop steroid abruptly — taper slowly
   - Any stress → stress dose

🚩 Waterhouse-Friderichsen syndrome
   - Bilateral adrenal hemorrhage + septic shock
   - Meningococcemia classic
   - Consider ใน sepsis ที่ refractory + petechiae

🚩 Etomidate + adrenal suppression
   - Single dose etomidate = adrenal suppression x 24-48 hr
   - ระวังใน septic patient
   - Consider alternative induction agent (ketamine)

🚩 Long-term management
   - Medical alert ID (จำเป็นมาก)
   - Emergency injection kit ที่บ้าน
   - Educate: stress dose rules, when to ER
   - Endocrine f/u ทุก 6-12 เดือน

🚩 Polyglandular AI syndromes
   - APS-1: adrenal + thyroid + hypoparathyroid
   - APS-2: adrenal + T1DM + thyroid
   - ตรวจ TSH, Ca, glucose
```

## 📊 การติดตาม (Monitoring)

**q 15–30 min (first hour):** BP, HR, SpO₂, mental status

**q 1 hr:** Glucose, neuro check, UO

**q 4–6 hr:** Electrolytes (Na, K), BUN/Cr

**q 12 hr:** CBC, Ca

**Daily:** Weight, fluid balance, cortisol level (if serial)

**Watch for:**
- **Persistent hypotension** → consider pressors (NE) + rule out other shock
- **Worsening mentation** → hyponatremia, hypoglycemia, sepsis
- **Arrhythmia** → K, Mg, Ca
- **Pulmonary edema** (over-resuscitation)

```
             [ ] dehydration [ ] trauma [ ] other: ___

1. HYDROCORTISONE 100 mg IV STAT
2. NSS 1 L IV rapid bolus (over 15-30 min)
3. Cardiac monitor, IV x 2
4. Blood glucose check → D50 50 mL IV if <70
5. Labs DURING/AFTER steroid: cortisol, ACTH, CBC, 
   lytes, glucose, BUN/Cr, Ca, blood cx x 2, UA, ECG, CXR

Maintenance (first 24 hr):
[ ] Hydrocortisone 50 mg IV q 6 hr (total 200 mg/day)
    OR drip 200 mg + NSS 50 mL → 2 mL/hr
[ ] NSS 200-250 mL/hr (2-3 L total Day 1)
[ ] D5NSS maintenance after glucose stable
[ ] Dextrose q 4-6 hr (monitor)

Treat precipitant:
[ ] Empiric antibiotic if infection suspect 
    (ceftriaxone 2 g IV + vancomycin if severe)
[ ] Treat specific cause

Hyperkalemia (if K > 6.5 or ECG changes):
[ ] Ca gluconate 10% 10 mL IV
[ ] Insulin R 10 U + D50 50 mL IV
[ ] Salbutamol NB 10 mg

Day 2-3 (taper):
- Hydrocortisone 100 mg/day IV once stable
- Transition to PO 15-25 mg/day in divided doses
- Add Fludrocortisone 0.1 mg PO daily (primary AI)

Discharge:
[ ] Education: stress dose rules, emergency kit
[ ] Medical alert ID
[ ] Endocrine f/u within 2 weeks
[ ] Return precautions (fever, vomiting, dizziness)

Monitor:
- BP, HR, mental status q 15-30 min initially
- Glucose q 1 hr x 4, then q 4 hr
- Electrolytes q 4-6 hr x 24 hr
- UO hourly
- Weight daily

Admit ICU / step-down
Endocrine consult
```
