## 🎯 Criteria / Diagnosis

### Diagnostic Criteria (ต้องมีอย่างน้อย 1)

**Criterion 1:** Acute onset (minutes-hours) **AND**
- Skin/mucosal symptoms (hives, itch, flush, swollen lips) **AND**
- **≥1 of:**
  - Respiratory: wheeze, stridor, SOB, hypoxia
  - Hypotension / end-organ dysfunction

**Criterion 2:** **≥2 of following** after likely allergen exposure:
- Skin/mucosal involvement
- Respiratory compromise
- Hypotension / collapse
- GI symptoms (vomiting, cramps)

**Criterion 3:** Hypotension after known allergen exposure
- SBP < 90 หรือ drop > 30% จาก baseline

### Common Triggers
- Drugs (antibiotics, NSAIDs, contrast)
- Food (seafood, nuts, eggs)
- Insect stings (bees, wasps)
- Latex

## ⚡ Immediate Actions (Act within seconds!)

### Priority 1: **EPINEPHRINE IM** (lifesaving!)

**Adults:**
- **0.3-0.5 mg IM** (1:1000)
- = 0.3-0.5 mL of 1:1000
- **Injection site: lateral thigh (vastus lateralis)** — best absorption
- Repeat q 5-15 min if no improvement

⚠️ **Common mistake:** เลื่อนให้ช้า หรือไม่ให้ IM → เสี่ยงเสียชีวิต!

### Priority 2: ABC Management

**A (Airway):**
- Assess for angioedema, stridor
- Prepare intubation (difficult airway!)
- Consider early intubation if progressing

**B (Breathing):**
- O₂ high flow (10-15 L/min mask)
- Nebulized β-agonist (Salbutamol) for bronchospasm

**C (Circulation):**
- 2 large-bore IV
- **NSS 1-2 L rapid bolus** (repeat as needed up to 4 L)
- Trendelenburg position if hypotension
- Monitor BP, HR continuous

### Priority 3: Remove Trigger
- Stop infusion if drug-related
- Remove stinger (ไม่ squeeze)
- Rinse mouth if oral trigger

### Priority 4: Adjunctive Therapy

**1. Antihistamine** (H1 blocker)
- Chlorpheniramine 10 mg IV slow
- หรือ Diphenhydramine 25-50 mg IV

**2. H2 blocker** (synergy with H1)
- Ranitidine 50 mg IV
- หรือ Famotidine 20 mg IV

**3. Corticosteroid** (prevent biphasic reaction)
- Hydrocortisone 100-200 mg IV
- หรือ Methylprednisolone 125 mg IV

⚠️ **Antihistamine + steroid ≠ adrenaline** — ให้ adrenaline ก่อน!

### Priority 5: Refractory Anaphylaxis
- **Adrenaline drip IV** (ถ้า IM ไม่ work)
- Glucagon 1-5 mg IV (ถ้าคนไข้ใช้ β-blocker)
- Consider vasopressin

## 💊 Drug Preparation (BW 60 kg)

### Epinephrine IM (FIRST-LINE)

```
Dose:     0.3-0.5 mg IM (1:1000)
Volume:   0.3-0.5 mL of 1:1000 ampule
Site:     LATERAL THIGH (vastus lateralis)
Repeat:   q 5-15 min if no response
```

**Why thigh not arm?**
- Better absorption (muscle mass)
- Faster onset
- Deltoid may fail in shock

---

### Epinephrine IV Drip (refractory)

```
Prep: Epinephrine 1 mg + NSS 100 mL
      = 10 mcg/mL
      
Start: 0.1 mcg/kg/min (≈ 6 mcg/min @ BW 60 kg)
       → 36 mL/hr

Titrate: 0.05-1 mcg/kg/min per BP
```

**⚠️ IV push:** use 1:10,000 (diluted), slow 0.5 mL increments
**Never IV push 1:1000!**

---

### Fluid Resuscitation

```
NSS 1-2 L rapid bolus (over 10-20 min)
Repeat 500 mL-1 L bolus prn
Up to 4 L in first hour

Monitor: BP, lung exam, UO
Stop if: pulmonary edema signs
```

---

### Chlorpheniramine (Antihistamine H1)

```
Dose:   10 mg IV slow (over 1 min)
        = 1 amp
Repeat: q 4-6 hr
```

**🫘 Renal (CKD):** ไม่ต้องปรับ
**🧓 Elderly:** ระวัง sedation, urinary retention

---

### Hydrocortisone

```
Dose:   100-200 mg IV q 6 hr
Role:   Prevent biphasic reaction (20% ของ anaphylaxis)
```

## ⚠️ Critical Pitfalls

```
🚩 Adrenaline เป็น first-line เสมอ
   - ไม่ใช่ antihistamine
   - ไม่ใช่ steroid
   - ถ้ามี criteria → IM adrenaline ทันที

🚩 IM NOT IV (เว้น refractory)
   - IM lateral thigh
   - IV push 1:1000 = fatal arrhythmia

🚩 Biphasic reaction (20%)
   - อาการกลับมาใน 1-72 ชม
   - Admit observe ≥ 6 hr (mild)
   - Admit ≥ 24 hr (severe/refractory)
   - ให้ steroid ก่อน discharge

🚩 Angioedema → intubate เร็ว
   - Airway อาจบวมใน 10-15 min
   - ถ้ารอ = intubate ยาก/ไม่ได้
   - Prepare cric

🚩 β-blocker user → refractory
   - Adrenaline ไม่ work เท่าปกติ
   - ใช้ Glucagon 1-5 mg IV

🚩 อย่าลืม EpiPen prescription ก่อน discharge
   - ให้คำสอน
   - Medical alert bracelet
   - Refer allergist
```

## 📝 Copy-Ready Order

```
1. Epinephrine 0.3-0.5 mg IM lateral thigh STAT,
   repeat q 5-15 min prn
2. O₂ high flow 10-15 L/min via non-rebreather
3. IV access × 2 (large bore 18G)
4. NSS 1-2 L IV bolus rapid, repeat 500 mL prn
5. Monitor ECG, SpO₂, BP continuous
6. If bronchospasm: Salbutamol NB 2.5 mg + O₂
7. Chlorpheniramine 10 mg IV slow
8. Ranitidine 50 mg IV
9. Hydrocortisone 200 mg IV
10. If refractory to IM epi:
    - Epinephrine drip 1 mg + NSS 100 mL
    - Start 5 mcg/min, titrate
11. If on β-blocker + refractory:
    - Glucagon 1-5 mg IV slow
12. Prepare airway: difficult intubation cart
13. CBC, CMP, ABG, tryptase level (baseline)
14. CXR
15. Admit for monitoring ≥ 24 hr (if severe)
16. EpiPen prescription + allergist referral
```
