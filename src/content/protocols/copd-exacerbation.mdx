## 🎯 Definition

**Acute worsening of respiratory symptoms** requiring additional therapy. Anthonisen criteria (≥ 2 = major):
1. ↑ Dyspnea
2. ↑ Sputum volume
3. ↑ Sputum purulence (yellow/green)

<Callout type="danger" title="Red flags — ICU candidate">

- **RR &gt; 30** + accessory muscle use + paradoxical abdominal motion
- **SpO₂ &lt; 90%** despite O₂
- **pH &lt; 7.25** (severe hypercapnic acidosis)
- **Altered mental status** (CO₂ narcosis)
- **Hemodynamic instability**

</Callout>

---

## 🧪 Workup

- **ABG** — pH, PaCO₂, PaO₂ (baseline often ↑ PaCO₂ in chronic; compare to baseline)
- **CXR** — r/o pneumonia, pneumothorax, pulmonary edema
- **ECG** — r/o MI, AFib
- **CBC, BMP, BNP, troponin** (co-morbidity eval)
- **Sputum G/S + C/S** if purulent or severe
- **Procalcitonin** (consider if unclear bacterial etiology)

---

## 💊 Treatment

### 1. Oxygen — careful titration!

<Callout type="warning" title="Target SpO₂ 88–92%">

ห้าม over-oxygenate — hyperoxia → V/Q mismatch + CO₂ retention → respiratory failure  
→ เริ่ม nasal cannula 1–2 L/min หรือ Venturi mask 24–28%  
→ Titrate to SpO₂ 88–92% (ไม่ใช่ 95%+)

</Callout>

### 2. Bronchodilators (short-acting)
- **Salbutamol 2.5–5 mg** nebulized + **Ipratropium 500 mcg** nebulized q 20 min × 3 → q 4–6 hr
- MDI + spacer ก็ได้ (ประสิทธิภาพเทียบเท่า nebulizer)

### 3. Systemic corticosteroids
- **Prednisolone 40 mg PO × 5 days** (REDUCE trial — same as 14 days)
- IV hydrocortisone 100 mg q 6 hr ถ้า cannot PO
- ไม่ต้อง taper ถ้า ≤ 5 days

### 4. Antibiotics — ให้ถ้ามี
- Anthonisen criteria ≥ 2 major (esp. purulent sputum)
- Mechanical ventilation (NIV or IMV)
- Severe exacerbation

**Empirical choices:**
| Scenario | Antibiotic |
|---|---|
| Outpatient, no RF | Amoxicillin-clavulanate 625 mg PO TID × 5–7 d |
| Inpatient, no RF | Ceftriaxone 1–2 g IV daily × 5–7 d |
| Risk Pseudomonas (recent abx, structural lung dz) | Pip-tazo 4.5 g IV q8h or Cefepime + azithromycin |
| MRSA risk | Add Vancomycin |

*Risk factors for Pseudomonas: FEV1 &lt; 50%, bronchiectasis, frequent abx, chronic steroid*

### 5. NIV (BiPAP) — **first-line for AECOPD with respiratory acidosis**

<Callout type="success" title="BiPAP ลด intubation + mortality">

**Indications:**
- pH 7.25–7.35 + PaCO₂ rising
- Respiratory distress despite optimal medical Rx
- **Earlier = better** (don't wait for pH &lt; 7.25)

**Settings:**
- IPAP 8–12, EPAP 4–5, FiO₂ titrate to SpO₂ 88–92%
- Titrate IPAP +2 q 20 min until RR &lt; 25, comfort, ABG improves
- Max IPAP 20 mostly
- **Reassess in 1–2 hr**: if no improvement → intubate

</Callout>

**Contraindications to NIV:**
- Respiratory arrest / apnea
- Impaired consciousness (can't protect airway)
- Hemodynamic instability
- Severe vomiting / facial trauma
- Uncooperative patient

### 6. IMV / Intubation — if NIV fails

**Settings for COPD:**
- Volume control or PRVC
- Vt 6–8 mL/kg IBW
- **Low RR** 10–12 (prolonged expiratory time)
- **I:E 1:3 to 1:5** (allow expiration, avoid auto-PEEP)
- PEEP 5 (watch auto-PEEP)
- **Permissive hypercapnia** acceptable (pH 7.20–7.25 OK)

**Risks:**
- Barotrauma (pneumothorax) — high plateau
- Auto-PEEP → hemodynamic compromise → may need to disconnect briefly + reduce RR

## 🏥 Disposition

### Discharge if
- Symptoms + ABG stable on oral regimen
- Able to eat, drink, sleep
- SpO₂ &gt; 90% on baseline O₂
- Understanding of new medications + return precautions

### Admit if
- Severe symptoms, poor functional baseline
- Failure of outpatient Rx
- NIV required
- Significant co-morbidities
- Inadequate home support

### Discharge instructions
- **Smoking cessation** counseling (most important)
- Inhaler technique review
- Influenza + pneumococcal vaccine
- Pulmonary rehabilitation referral
- COPD action plan

---

## ⚠️ Pitfalls

<Callout type="danger" title="COPD Pitfalls">

**Over-oxygenation** — SpO₂ 99% on O₂ = CO₂ retention risk  
→ Target 88–92%, use Venturi mask for precise FiO₂

**Delay NIV** — waiting until pH &lt; 7.25 delays benefit  
→ Start BiPAP when pH 7.30 + symptoms not improving

**ไม่ให้ steroid** — evidence clear: systemic steroid shortens exacerbation  
→ Prednisolone 40 mg × 5 days ทุกรายที่ moderate/severe

**Wrong ventilator settings** — high RR → breath stacking → auto-PEEP → cardiac arrest  
→ Low RR (10–12), long expiratory time, plateau monitoring

**Miss underlying trigger** — Pneumonia, PE, MI, pneumothorax  
→ CXR + ECG + trop + consider CT-PA if D-dimer high

</Callout>
